The Challenge

The Silent Threat to Pet Vision

Many ocular conditions in pets, such as glaucoma and cataracts, often go undetected until they reach advanced stages, leading to irreversible vision loss.

Subtle Early Symptoms

Early symptoms are frequently subtle and easily missed by pet owners, delaying critical treatment.

Gaps Between Check-ups

Regular veterinary check-ups may not catch rapidly progressing conditions between visits.

Invasive Diagnostics

Traditional diagnostic methods are often invasive or stressful for animals.

No Continuous Monitoring

Lack of continuous monitoring means critical data points are missed.

The emotional and financial toll on pet owners can be substantial, emphasizing the urgent need for a proactive solution.

Our Solution

Introducing the SANS Collar

A non-invasive, smart device designed for continuous ocular health monitoring in pets. A paradigm shift from reactive treatment to proactive, preventative eye care.

Wearable Comfort

Lightweight and ergonomic, ensuring maximum comfort for pets of all sizes, from small cats to large dogs.

Non-Invasive Scanning

Utilizes proprietary low-power light scanning technology to painlessly assess ocular parameters without direct contact.

Real-Time Data

Provides continuous monitoring of key indicators, transmitting data directly to a user-friendly mobile application.

Early Alert System

Core Innovation

Advanced Biometric Sensing

Our core innovation lies in the sophisticated biometric sensing capabilities embedded within the SANS Collar.

Advanced Light Spectrum Analysis

Utilizes a unique blend of specific light wavelengths to penetrate and analyze ocular tissues, detecting subtle changes invisible to the naked eye.

Micro-Vascular Flow Detection

Monitors blood flow changes in the retinal and corneal micro-vasculature, early indicators of inflammatory or degenerative conditions.

Intraocular Pressure Estimation

Leverages AI algorithms to non-invasively estimate intraocular pressure (IOP), a critical factor in glaucoma diagnosis, without direct contact or puff-test methods.

Neural Network Processing

On-device AI interprets complex sensor data, identifies patterns indicative of disease, and filters out false positives, ensuring reliable alerts.

This comprehensive approach allows for unprecedented early detection of conditions like glaucoma, uveitis, and even certain types of ocular tumors, giving pets the best chance for successful treatment.
